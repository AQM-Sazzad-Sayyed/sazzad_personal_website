Deep Learning for Human Activity Recognition

Advisor: [Add your advisor's name here]

Duration: [Start Year] - Present

My doctoral research focuses on developing novel deep neural network architectures for human activity recognition using multimodal wearable sensor data. The research addresses key challenges including noise in time-series data, complex inter-modal relationships among sensors, and automated feature extraction from raw sensor inputs.

Key contributions include developing multi-stage training approaches that improve recognition accuracy while handling the complexity of real-world sensor data. This work has applications in healthcare monitoring, fitness tracking, and assistive technologies.
