Conference Papers

A Novel Multi-Stage Training Approach for Human Activity Recognition From Multimodal Wearable Sensor Data Using Deep Neural Network

Tanvir Mahmud, AQM Sazzad Sayyed, Shaikh Anowarul Fattah, Sun-Yuan Kung

IEEE Sensors Journal, August 2020

Deep neural networks are effective for automatically recognizing human actions using data from wearable sensors. This paper presents a novel multi-stage training approach that addresses challenges of noisy time-series data and complex inter-modal relationships among sensors. The method improves feature extraction and recognition accuracy through systematic training stages.
